About Me

Hello! My name is Erik Larsson I live in Stockholm, Sweden and I'm 18 years old. I was born in Singapore, and I've also lived in Thailand and Belgium. I play rugby, like racing, dabble in basketball and am on the grind to become the middle weight champion of the world in boxing. I like making music and videos with my friends and I guess I'm just a pretty cool guy in general.

Useful Stuff

I can work my way around three DAW's: Logic Pro, Pro Tools and Ableton. I'm also familliar with Final Cut Pro on the video editing side of things. My main instrument is the guitar, but I also play the piano, bass and drums. All my music is played, mixed and mastered by myself as well. The vocals in my music are all me, but sometimes I like to mix in my friends' voices to add a bit of character. For more on the individual songs themselves, check out the music page. There you can see how I made them, and the thought process behind them.
